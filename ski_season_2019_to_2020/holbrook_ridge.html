Holbrook Ridge

There is sometimes just one day during the entire winter when it pukes so much white stuff that you can actually ski some of the low-elevation terrain. On one such occation, I decided to ski the north-ridge of Holbrook Canyon.

The video shows the whole descent in un-edited, pain-staking detail. A good skier (like John), I imagine, could have zig-zagged down the whole thing with skis parallel, even the steep narrow parts of the trail. Knowing I would certainly crash if I attempted such a thing, I just eeked my way down.
